Captive 101

What is Captive Insurance?

A specialized form of self-insurance that empowers businesses to take a proactive and strategic approach to managing their risks.

Specialized Self-Insurance

Captive insurance companies are owned and controlled by the organizations they insure, providing a tailored approach to risk management.

Greater Control

Captive insurance allows businesses to have more control over their insurance policies, coverage, and risk management strategies.

Customized Solutions

Design insurance coverage that specifically addresses your unique risk exposures and requirements, without paying for unnecessary coverage.

Key Benefits

Rising Premiums

U.S. commercial lines premiums increased ~3.8% year-over-year in Q2 2025, with excess/umbrella liability and commercial auto seeing sharper increases.

Market Volatility

Carriers tighten underwriting, raise deductibles, exclude or limit coverage for emerging risks, and demand more stringent terms.

Rising Costs

Inflation, rising materials and labor costs, and increased catastrophe exposure drive up claim costs, pressuring underwriters to raise rates.

Coverage Gaps

Coverage may be constrained or unavailable in the traditional market, especially for unique, emerging, or hard-to-insure exposures.
